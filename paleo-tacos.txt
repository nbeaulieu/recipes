Paleo Tacos:

3-4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
Coconut oil
Romaine lettuce heart leaves
Cherry tomatoes (organic)
Avocado

Slice the chicken into strips
Spice as desired
Cook chicken in coconut oil in cast iorn pan

While chicken is cooking, slice tomatoes and avocado.
Wash lettuce leaves and pat dry.

When chicken is cooked, place strips of chicken in a lettuce leave, add 
tomatoes and avocado.  YUM!
